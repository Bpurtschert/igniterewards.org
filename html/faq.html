Frequently Asked Questions

What is Ignite Rewards?
Ignite Rewards is a platform designed to help schools motivate students through a points-based reward system integrated into classroom tools like Canvas LMS.
How do I integrate Ignite with Canvas?
You can follow our Canvas Integration Guide, which includes step-by-step instructions and helpful visuals for setting up LTI access.
Who can I contact for support?
You can email us at help@igniterewards.org or call (801) 747-9823 during support hours (Monday–Friday, 9am–5pm MST).
Is Ignite Rewards free for schools?
We offer custom pricing based on school size and feature needs. Contact us for a quote or partnership discussion.
Will rewards hurt students’ intrinsic motivation over time?
Absolutely not. Ignite Rewards is designed to complement, not replace, intrinsic motivation. Our platform encourages short-term engagement through positive reinforcement while also fostering long-term habits and personal growth. Teachers lead with their own classroom values — we simply offer tools to support them.
What kind of rewards are used — candy, toys, or something else?
Schools can fully customize the rewards. Many prefer non-material incentives like extra recess, job privileges, or time with a friend. Others choose practical or meaningful items such as school gear, books, or activity passes. The system is flexible and can reflect the values of your specific school.
How are students recognized — by improvement or consistency?
Ignite supports both. Teachers can choose to reward students for consistent positive behavior or for showing growth and improvement over time. The system provides flexibility so educators can reinforce effort, achievement, or change — whatever best aligns with your classroom goals.
Who manages the program at the school level?
Typically, each school designates a point person — such as a counselor, admin, or teacher leader. However, Ignite Rewards is designed to be self-sustaining and low-overhead. We provide onboarding, training, and automation tools so the program is simple to manage without becoming a burden.
How are rewards delivered — do we have to store or ship items?
You have options. Rewards can be digital (like privileges or printable coupons), physical (managed by your school), or hybrid. Some schools choose to stock their own prize inventory, while others partner with us to receive pre-packed quarterly kits. We’ll help you find the solution that fits your space, staffing, and budget.
