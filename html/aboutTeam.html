Jaxon Leatham

Founding Partner — Marketing & Finance

Jaxon brings strategic clarity and financial insight to Ignite Rewards. As a founding partner, he helped shape the foundation of our mission to uplift students through incentive-driven learning.

A proud graduate of Ensign College of Business and a product of Utah’s public school system, Jaxon has a unique understanding of the education landscape. His service as a missionary for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints instilled a deep sense of purpose and empathy.

At Ignite, he is passionate about building systems that are not only economically sustainable, but socially impactful—ensuring that every decision aligns with the greater good.

Milo Kersey

Founding Partner — Sales & Key Partnerships

Milo, a founding partner of Ignite Rewards, is the engine behind our expanding network of partnerships. With over eight years of experience in sales and relationship development, he knows how to build bridges that last.

Growing up in Utah’s school system and graduating from Ensign Business College gave him firsthand insight into the challenges students and educators face. His commitment to self-growth and professionalism fuels his drive to create authentic, long-term connections.

Milo believes every school deserves innovative tools that empower both teachers and students to thrive.

Brayden Purtschert

Founding Partner — IT & Software Development

As a founding partner and lead developer at Ignite Rewards, Brayden is the technical mind behind the platform’s functionality and impact. With six years of software engineering experience and a BA in the field, he brings both expertise and vision to our mission.

Brayden is passionate about using technology to create opportunity—especially for students who feel left behind. He believes the right tools can inspire, motivate, and change lives.

For him, Ignite isn’t just software—it’s a way to level the playing field and unlock potential in every classroom.
